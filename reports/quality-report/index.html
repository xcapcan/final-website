What does Quality Assurance mean for web site development?

Quality Assurance makes sure that when during the web site development, back end user’s experience is considered during the creation and ensures that there are no flaws that were overlooked during the development which could possibly create troubles for the users.

Describe the two key principles of Quality Assurance: "Fit for Purpose" and "Right First Time."

Fit for purpose - meaning the product or service meets its intended purpose. Right first time - any mistakes are immediately dealt with.

If the website meets all the basic web standards, it’s quality can be considered sufficient.

Describe what Web Accessibility means? What steps you can take to make your pages more accessible?

Web accessibility is the inclusive practice of ensuring there are no barriers that prevent interaction with, or access to, websites on the World Wide Web by people with physical disabilities, situational disabilities, and socio-economic restrictions on bandwidth and speed. When sites are correctly designed, developed and edited, generally all users have equal access to information and functionality. To make sure your website is accessible to all users, you should be thoughtful of your design and development and consider things like screen readers for example and make sure your website is functional with that software.
